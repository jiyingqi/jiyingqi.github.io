Rome was ok

Until the last couple days

Posted by Jiying Qi on August, 26, 2017

There's this one museum which really screwed us over.

Of course it's not the museums fault or anything but, I still blame it for all our problems in Rome.

We had a 3 day pass of Rome which meant unlimited bus fare, free admission to two museums or sites, and reduced admission for anywhere else.

On our first day we visited the collisseum and it was pretty cool.

Then, we went to the Borgheiss Museum which is like the top dog art museum in Rome.

When we arrived, around 3 PM, the lady in the ticket booth told us we could only reserve tickets over the phone.

So we left and sat at a random desolate bus station for half an hour.

There was a stable of horses right next to it, so I just passed time neighing to the horses and watching their reactions.

But the bus never came so we just started walking back and did some other things.

On day two we went back to the Borgheiss Museum super early cuz we didn't even know the phone number to reserve tickets and maybe possibly reserve the tickets there.

Unfortunately their tickets were all sold out by 9 AM for that day so we were like, "wtf is with this museum".

We talked to a different ticket attendent and this time we actually bought tickets there for 3 PM entrance to the museum for tomorrow.

The phone wasn't even needed?!?!? So that meant that the first day attendent lied to our faces

Ok so then came the third day.

We got on the bus to the Borgheiss Museum but we didnt get off at the Borgheiss Museum.

Because some dude got on the bus, stole my dads wallet with the tickets and ran off.

Nobody even noticed till my dad tried to check the tickets because the busses there are jampacked with people.

Apaarently, this is a frequent thing that happens in Rome?!?! Sigh..

And to make matters worse this also cancelled all our future plans.

My parents had to freeze all their credit cards and my dad also lost his driver liscense and his EU drivers liscense.

We were planning to drive to Pompeii on the last day but couldn't get the rental car because of that.

Thankfully, I brought my credit card, which is also a travel rewards card so that means no foreign transaction fees, and we used that for the last couple of days.

Whatever, i'm glad that i'm going back home soon.
